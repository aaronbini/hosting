Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Hosting Helper ("we", "our", or "us") is a meal planning tool that helps you plan menus and shopping lists for events. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Information We Collect

When you sign in with Google, we receive:

  • Your name and email address (used to identify your account)
  • A profile picture URL (displayed in the app)

If you choose to connect Google Tasks or Google Sheets, we request access to those services solely to deliver your shopping list to them. We do not read or store any pre-existing data from your Tasks or Sheets.

We also store the event details and meal plans you enter in the app so you can return to them later.

How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and associate your plans with your account
  • To create Google Tasks lists or Google Sheets on your behalf, only when you explicitly request it
  • To save and retrieve your event planning sessions

We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising.

Google API Scopes

Hosting Helper's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy , including the Limited Use requirements.

We request the following Google OAuth scopes:

  • openid, email, profile — to identify you when you sign in
  • Google Tasks — to create a shopping list task in your Google Tasks, only when you choose this output option
  • Google Sheets — to create a shopping list spreadsheet in your Google Drive, only when you choose this output option

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in a secure database hosted on Google Cloud. We retain your event plans until you delete them. You can delete your data at any time from within the app.

Third-Party Services

We use Google Gemini to generate meal plans and shopping lists. Input you provide (event details, guest counts, dietary needs) is sent to Google's AI service to produce responses. Please review Google's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle this data.
